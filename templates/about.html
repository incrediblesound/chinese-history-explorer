Chinese History Explorer

~*~

This site is an open-source wiki about Chinese history. You can add to or edit the content by making pull-requests on the source repo on github.

The philosophy behind this site is to provide concise summaries of specific topics in Chinese history. The ideal topic will cut a balance between being too general to be interesting and being bogged down in details. A person should be able to read about a topic and have a good idea about what that topic is, and all complexity within the topic should be broken out into sub-topics.
